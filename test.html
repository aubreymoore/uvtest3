Methods

This presentation documents my method for extracting wingbeat waveforms from Smith's video using the following steps.

  1. Download the YouTube video.
  2. Extract individual frames from the video.
  3. Generate the wingbeat waveform by calculating the brightness of each frame.
  4. Analyze the waveform to measure amplitudes of the wingbeat frequency and harmonics.
